EA update

EA has recently disclosed fresh information about its upcoming 2023 soccer title, EA Sports FC. This marks the inception of a new game series that distances itself from the FIFA branding, following the conclusion of its association with the international soccer governing body last year. Despite this rebranding, EA Sports FC will retain a familiar roster of players, teams, and stadiums through its existing licensing agreements.


Nick Wlodyka, representing EA Sports FC, conveyed, "We're building on 30 years of leadership and history creating experiences that bring the global football community together, and continuing to take it into a fan-first future," EA Sports FC's Nick Wlodyka said in a press statement. "EA Sports FC will be a symbol for the sport, a symbol of innovation and change, and we're energized to show our fans more about the future in July."
